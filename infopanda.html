Fun Facts About Movie

Animators collaborated with martial arts experts and studied real fight footage to ensure Po’s movements were true to Kung Fu. The film also pays tribute to Chinese culture through traditional painting styles, architecture, and the theme of inner peace and spiritual balance.

Animating Po was a challenge—his clumsy, fluffy form had to move with convincing Kung Fu agility. Animators spent months balancing humor with fluid action. Meanwhile, Jack Black took Kung Fu lessons to better channel Po’s spirit and bring energy to his voice performance.

Fun Facts About Movie

Nicolas Cage, who voiced the overprotective dad Grug, often recorded his lines while wearing pajamas in his home studio, bringing a relaxed and quirky energy to the character. Interestingly, although Eep and Guy shared a strong on-screen bond, their voice actors—Emma Stone and Ryan Reynolds—never recorded together; all their chemistry was crafted in post-production editing.

The Croods’ movement and expressions were influenced by physical comedy and early silent films, with animators studying slapstick techniques to bring primal humor to life. The film’s emotional depth was also a major focus; the father-daughter conflict between Grug and Eep added heart to the adventure, reflecting themes of change, independence, and family growth.
